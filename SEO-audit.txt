# SEO Audit Report: artacestudio.com
Audit Date: 2026-04-16
Site: https://artacestudio.com/
Audit Type: Full Site Audit

## Executive Summary

Artace Studio has a strong visual foundation with high-quality product imagery and a clear value proposition as a handmade canvas painting studio based in Pune, India. The site implements basic SEO best practices including a sitemap and proper robots.txt configuration.

**Biggest Strength**: Excellent product catalog with deep niche categories (Radha Krishna, Buddha, Ganesha paintings) that have high commercial intent.

**Top 3 Priorities**:
1.  Fix missing meta descriptions and optimize title tags across all pages
2.  Add descriptive alt text to all product images
3.  Implement schema markup for products and articles

**Overall Assessment**: Strong foundation with significant opportunity for improvement. The site currently lacks critical on-page optimization elements that are preventing it from ranking for high-intent commercial keywords.
